Kaneyoshi Izumi (和泉 かねよし, Izumi Kaneyoshi, born April 1) is a Japanese manga artist. Her debut short story, "Tenshi" (lit. "Angel"), was published in the September 1995 issue of Bessatsu Shōjo Comic magazine and won the 36th Shogakukan Shinjin Comic Award. Izumi also won the Shogakukan Manga Award in the shōjo (girls') manga category in 2006 for Sonnanja neyo and in 2015 for Joō no Hana.
