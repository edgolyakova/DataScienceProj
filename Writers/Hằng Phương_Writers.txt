Hằng Phương (Điện Bàn, 9 September 1908 – 2 February 1983) was a Vietnamese poet. Her poetry has been characterised as intimist.She was born into an educated Confucian family, and married writer Vũ Ngọc Phan. Her daughter is the painter Vũ Giáng Hương.
