Mile Nedelkoski (Macedonian: Миле Неделкоски) (November 25, 1935 – June 21, 2020) was a Macedonian poet, novelist, storyteller, and playwright. In North Macedonia, he had a reputation for being a bulgarophile intellectual. Biography Nedelkoski was born on November 25, 1935 in Prilep. He served as an editor at Kultura press. He became a member of the Macedonian Writers' Society (Macedonian: Друштво на писателите на Македонија, ДПМ) in 1963. Awards that he received included the Miladinov Brothers Prize, the Kočo Racin Prize, and the Racin Award. Nedelkoski died on June 21, 2020 in Skopje.
