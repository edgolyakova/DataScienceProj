Pamela Stirling is a New Zealand journalist and editor. In 2004 she was appointed editor of New Zealand Listener magazine. Biography Stirling was a journalist for the New Zealand Listener for sixteen years before becoming editor in 2004. As a journalist, she received research scholarships to Cambridge and Stanford Universities.
