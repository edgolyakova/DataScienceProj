Donncha Ó Cróinín (8 December 1919 – 5 June 1990) was an Irish scholar. He was the son of the sean-nós singer Elizabeth Cronin and the father of the historian Dáibhí Ó Cróinín. Publications Dillon, Myles; Ó Cróinín, Donncha (1961). Teach Yourself Irish. London: English Universities Press. References
