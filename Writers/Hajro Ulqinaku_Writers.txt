Hajro Ulqinaku is an Albanian writer from Ulcinj, Montenegro. Life Hajro was born on 3 May 1938 to an Albanian family in Ulcinj, Montenegro. He finished the primary and secondary school in Ulcinj and the university studies in Universiteti i Prishtinës in Pristina, Kosovo.
