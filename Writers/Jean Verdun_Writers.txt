Jean Verdun (21 March 1931 – 21 October 2021) was a French writer.

Biography
Jean's father, Henri Verdun, was a judge who was killed in 1944. He was the nephew of politician André Diethelm. Verdun wrote numerous novels and plays on Freemasonry in France. From 1985 to 1988, he was Grand Master of the Grande Loge de France. In 1997, he left the Grand Orient de France, but returned in 2019.Verdun died on 21 October 2021 at the age of 90.
