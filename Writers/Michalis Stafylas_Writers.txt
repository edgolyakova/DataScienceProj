Michalis Stafylas (Greek: Μιχάλης Σταφυλάς) ( 1920 - 22 May 2018) was born in Granitsa, Evrytania in Greece.  He has published biographical and critical work about 50 modern Greek writers, essays, novels, anthologies of stories, poetry anthologies and a theatrical play.  His work has been translated into many languages.
