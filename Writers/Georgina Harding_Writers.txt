Georgina Harding is an English author of fiction. Published works include her novels The Spy Game (shortlisted for The Encore Award 2011) and Painter of Silence.She has also written two works of non-fiction: Tranquebar: A Season in South India and In Another Europe. She lives in London and the Stour Valley, Essex.
