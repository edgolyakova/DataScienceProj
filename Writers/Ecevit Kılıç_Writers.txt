Ecevit Kılıç (born 1977) is a Turkish journalist and writer. He was a columnist for Cumhuriyet (1999 - 2006) and later Sabah. He is the author of a number of books on Turkish organised crime (including its involvement in football) and the Turkish deep state (including the Special Warfare Department and JİTEM).
