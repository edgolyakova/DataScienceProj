Miquel Creus i Muñoz alias Miquel Desclot (Barcelona, 1952) is a Spanish writer and translator known for his Catalan translations of works by Dante, Petrarca or Michelangelo Buonarroti. He was one of the founders of the Catalan literary collective, Ofèlia Dracs around 1976.
