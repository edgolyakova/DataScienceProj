Hédi Balegh (Arabic: بالغ, الهادي; died 10 November 2022) was a Tunisian academic, writer, journalist, television and radio presenter, and translator.Balegh was an associate professor of French at the Faculty of Letters, Arts and Humanities at Manouba University. In the 1990s, he published Proverbes tunisiens in three volumes. He was credited with translating The Little Prince by Antoine de Saint-Exupéry into Tunisian Arabic.
