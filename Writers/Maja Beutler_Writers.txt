Maja Beutler (8 December 1936 – 15 December 2021) was a Swiss writer. Career She worked as a translator and for Stadttheater Bern and Schweizer Radio DRS, and wrote in German. Beutler died on 15 December 2021, at the age of 85. Works Flissingen fehlt auf der Karte. Geschichten.
