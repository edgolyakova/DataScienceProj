Abd al-Rahman Abd al-Khaliq (Arabic: عبد الرحمن عبد الخالق) (5 November 1939 – 29 September 2020) was an Egyptian-Kuwaiti Islamic scholar and preacher. He published more than 60 books related to Islam, especially Salafism. Life Abd al-Khaliq was born in Monufia, Egypt, on 5 November 1939. He was educated at Islamic University of Madinah. He moved to Kuwait in 1965, because he was a teacher in Kuwaiti schools between 1965 and 1990. Then, he became an Islamic scholar at Society of the Revival of Islamic Heritage in Kuwait. On 31 October 2011, amiri decree was issued to give him Kuwaiti nationality.Abd al-Khaliq died on 29 September 2020 in Kuwait City, Kuwait, due to myocardial infarction.
