Dragoslav Mihailović (Serbian Cyrillic: Драгослав Михаиловић; 17 November 1930 – 12 March 2023) was a Serbian writer.

Life
Mihailović graduated in Yugoslav literature from the University of Belgrade in 1957 and was a member of the Serbian Academy of Sciences and Arts since 1981. In 1950 he was arrested and then imprisoned 15 months at Goli otok.Mihailović lived in Belgrade. He died on 12 March 2023, at the age of 92.
