Florjan Lipuš (born 4 May 1937 in Lobnig above Bad Eisenkappel, Austria) is a Carinthian Slovene writer and translator. Since 1985 he has been a corresponding member of the Slovenian Academy of Sciences and Arts. Awards 2004: Prešeren Award 2011: Petrarca-Preis 2013: Franz Nabl Prize 2018: Grand Austrian State Prize 2022: Honorary Doctor, University of Klagenfurt 2022: Grand Decoration of Honour in Gold, state of Carinthia References
