Juan Rodríguez Pérez (born Sauce, Peru 1952) is a Peruvian writer of short novels. He studied sociology at the National University of San Marcos, and has been published in the magazines El Ñandú Desplumado, El Narrador, and Casa de Asterión.Pérez's work is notable for its themes about the Peruvian Amazon.
