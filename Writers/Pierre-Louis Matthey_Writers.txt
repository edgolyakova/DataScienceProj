Biography The French-speaking Swiss poet and writer Pierre-Louis Matthey (1893 - 1970) is considered one of the most important Swiss-French poets. His themes explore the passions and despair of adolescence, mythology and the absolute powers of art, describing a poetic journey from youth to maturity.
