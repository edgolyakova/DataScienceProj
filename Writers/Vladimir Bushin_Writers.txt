Vladimir Sergeyevich Bushin (Russian: Влади́мир Серге́евич Бу́шин; 24 January 1924 – 25 December 2019) was a Russian writer, essayist, literary critic, columnist and social activist. Bushin was born in Glukhovo, Bogorodsky Uyezd, Moscow Governorate, RSFSR, USSR, and was a member of the Union of Soviet Writers and Communist.
