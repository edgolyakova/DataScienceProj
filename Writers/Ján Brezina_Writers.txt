Ján Brezina (1 January 1917 in Východná – 4 August 1997 in Bratislava) was a Slovak poet, literary historian and theoretician. He was an employee of the Slovak Academy of Sciences since 1943, of the Slovak Matica and from 1970 - 1973 director of the Institute for Literal History of the Slovak Academy of Sciences. Works Poems: Nikdy sa nestretnem (I will never meet) Okrídlený deň (A day with wings) Nočné bdenie (Night waking) Horúcou linkou (By a hotline)Literary history: Slovenská poézia v revolučných rokoch 1917-1922 (Slovak poetry in the revolutionary years 1917-1922)
