David Cavanagh was an Irish writer and music journalist, best known for his the critically acclaimed 2000 book My Magpie Eyes Are Hungry for the Prize, which detailed the rise and fall of Creation Records, and for his editorship of Select magazine in the 1990s. Cavanagh was born in Dublin, and grew up in Northern Ireland. During his career, he wrote for Sounds, Select, Q, Uncut and Mojo.Cavanagh killed himself by jumping in front of a train in Luton in December 2018, aged 54.
