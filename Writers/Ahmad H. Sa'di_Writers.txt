Ahmad Sa'di (Arabic: احمد سعدي‎; Hebrew: אחמד סעדי; Born 1958) is a Palestinian social scientist and a tenured professor in the Department of Politics and Government in Ben-Gurion University of the Negev. He is the author of multiple books. Most notably he co-authored Nakba: Palestine, 1948, and the Claims of Memory (2007) with Lila Abu-Lughod.
