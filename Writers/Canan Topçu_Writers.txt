Canan Topçu (born 1965, Bursa, Turkey) is a Turkish-German writer. She arrived in Germany in 1973 and studied literature at Leibniz University Hannover. Her works deal mainly with migration and integration. Since 2003, she has been working as a professor at Hochschule Darmstadt. Works May Ayim, Canan Topçu (Red.): …aus dem Inneren der Sprache.
