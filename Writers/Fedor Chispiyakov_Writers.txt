Fedor Stepanovich Chispiyakov(Russian cyrillic: Фёдор Степанович Чиспияков, Mezhdurechensk, Kemerovo Oblast, 1906-1978) was a Soviet writer. He is described as the father of the Shor language literature in the books История Кузбасса (History of Kuzbass) and Сказки Сибири и Севера (Tales of Siberia and the North).He studied in Moscow and came back to Siberia to work as a teacher.
