Robert Abirached (25 August 1930 – 15 July 2021) was a French writer and theatrologist. He moved to Paris in 1948 and was admitted to the École normale supérieure in 1952. He earned a doctoral degree from the Sorbonne in 1974. Principal works Écrivains d’aujourd’hui (1940-1960) (1960) Casanova ou la dissipation (1961) L’Emerveillée (1963) Tu connais la musique?
