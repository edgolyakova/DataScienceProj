Babken Simonyan (Armenian: Բաբկեն Սիմոնյան, Serbian: Бабкен Симоњан, romanized: Babken Simonjan, born 20 March 1952) is an Armenian-Serbian poet, essayist, translator, university professor and diplomat currently serving as the honorary consul of Serbia in Armenia since 2006.He is a member of the Writers Union of Armenia, Association of Writers of Serbia and Adligat. He teaches Serbian language at the Yerevan State University.
