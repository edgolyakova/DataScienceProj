Yu Kikuta (菊田 悠, Kikuta Yū, born November 13, 1979), better known as Kick (キック, Kikku, originally as KICK☆), is a Japanese comedian and writer who is represented by the talent agency, Horipro. He was born in Tokyo, and graduated from Toho Junior and Senior High School and Japan University of the Arts Faculty Department of Photography.
