Jaime Zevallos is a Peruvian-American actor and writer. He is also of Spanish and German Jewish descent. Early life Jaime Zevallos was born in Peru. His family moved to New York when he was three. Career In 2018, Jaime Zevallos joined the cast of Marvel's Cloak & Dagger as Father Delgado, an ally to the titular characters.In September 2020, Zevallos was cast along with Zachary Laoutides and Alexander James Rodriguez in the upcoming film "Where Sweet Dreams Die."
