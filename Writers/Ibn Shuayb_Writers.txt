Abu l-`Abbas Ahmad ibn Muhammad ibn Shuayb al-Kirjani, known as Ibn Shuayb or Ibn Suhayb (Arabic: ابن شعيب; died 1 March 1349) was a Moroccan scholar of medicine, alchemy, botany, astronomy, mathematics, a poet,  and the chancellor of the Marinid sultan Abu al Hassan. He was born in Taza, and died in Tunis. References Sources Velázquez Basanta, F. N. (2017). "Ibn Suhayb, Abu L-`Ala'". Biblioteca de Al-Andalus (in Spanish). Vol. 5 (Lugar y Fecha ed.).
