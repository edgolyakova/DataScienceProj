Chote Praepan (Thai: โชติ แพร่พันธุ์, RTGS: Chot Phraephan, 15 May 1907 – 5 April 1956), known by his pen name Jacob (ยาขอบ, RTGS: Yakhop), was a Thai writer. His most famous work is Phu Chana Sip Thit (The Man Who Gained Victory in Ten Directions). He died of tuberculosis in 1956.  In 2018 Google ran a Google Doodle in his honor. == References ==
