Simon Loxley is a British graphic designer and bestselling author. Life He was born and raised in London. Career He has worked for the National Portrait Gallery, Museum of London, the Geffrye Museum, the Maritime Museum, the London Library, and the Dulwich Picture Gallery.He is a founding editor of Ultrabold magazine.
