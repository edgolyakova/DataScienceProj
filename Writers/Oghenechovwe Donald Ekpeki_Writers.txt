Oghenechovwe Donald Ekpeki or Ekpeki Oghenechovwe Donald (born 19 January) is a Nigerian speculative fiction writer, editor and publisher. His fiction and non-fiction have appeared in Omenana Magazine, Cosmic Roots and Eldritch Shores, Tor.com, Strange Horizons, Uncanny Magazine, NBC and more.
