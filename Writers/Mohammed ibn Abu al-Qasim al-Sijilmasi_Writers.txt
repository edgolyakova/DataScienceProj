Abu Abd Allah Mohammed ibn Abi al-Qasim al-Sijilmasi (Arabic: محمد بن أبي القاسم السجلماسي) was a Moroccan Maliki scholar. He is especially well known for his Sharh al-amal al-mutlaq: al-musammá bi-Fath al-jalīl al-samad fī sharh al-takmīl wa-al-mutamad. It was finished in 1782.
