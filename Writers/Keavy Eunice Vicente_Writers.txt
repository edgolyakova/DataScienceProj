Keavy Eunice Vicente is a Filipino screenwriter. She is most famous for writing Pearl Next Door, a girls love spin off of the Filipino boys love web series Gameboys. Education and personal life Vicente attended and finished high school at Miriam College in 2007. She finished her journalism degree at the University of Santo Tomas, Manila in 2011.
