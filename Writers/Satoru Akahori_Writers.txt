Satoru Akahori (あかほり さとる, Akahori Satoru, born 赤堀 悟, March 8, 1965) is a Japanese scriptwriter, novelist and manga author. He is best known for the Saber Marionette, Sakura Wars and Sorcerer Hunters series, which comes in anime, novel and manga forms.
