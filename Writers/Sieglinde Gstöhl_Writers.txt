Sieglinde Gstöhl (born 1964) is an academic from Liechtenstein.

Biography
She currently serves as Director of Studies of the department of EU International Relations and Diplomacy Studies at the College of Europe in Bruges, Belgium, as well as a professor of international relations. Before joining the College she was assistant professor of international relations from 1999 to 2005 at the Institute of Social Sciences at Humboldt University of Berlin.She studied economics and social sciences at the University of St. Gallen, where she graduated in 1988. She later studied International Relations and Political Sciences at the Graduate Institute of International Studies in Geneva. She previously was a member of the Centre of International Affairs at Harvard University in Cambridge, Massachusetts.
