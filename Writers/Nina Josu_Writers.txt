Nina Josu (born February 17, 1953 in Țiganca) is a writer and activist from Moldova. She is the head of the Association for Romanian Literature and Culture "ASTRA" - "Onisifor Ghibu".

Biography
Nina Josu graduated from Maxim Gorky Literature Institute in 1976. She is a member of the Moldovan Writers' Union. Nina Josu works for Literatura și Arta.Nina Josu is president of the Association for Romanian Literature and Culture "ASTRA" - "Onisifor Ghibu" (Romanian: Asociaţia pentru Literatura şi Cultura Română „Astra" – "O.Ghibu), Chişinău (reactivated on November 17, 1994). She is a leader of Democratic Forum of Romanians in Moldova.
