Abu Zaid Abd-Arrahman Ibn Ali Salih al-Makudi (Arabic: عبد الرحمن المكودي; died in Fes, in 1405) was a grammarian from Morocco. His Sharh al Makudi, is a commentary to the Grammar Alfiyya of Ibn Malik, with the glosses of Almellewi printed in the margin. His commentary is of great value for the study of grammar.
