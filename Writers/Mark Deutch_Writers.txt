Mark Mikhaylovich Deutch (Russian: Марк Михайлович Дейч, also transliterated as Deutsch, Deich and Deitch; 26 January 1945 – 2 May 2012) was a Russian journalist and columnist for Moskovskij Komsomolets. He became widely known while working with Radio Free Europe/Radio Liberty. He authored several books on politics.
