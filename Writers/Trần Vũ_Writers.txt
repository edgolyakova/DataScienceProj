Trần Vũ (Saigon 2 October 1962) is a Vietnamese-language writer living in France.Vũ arrived in Paris via a boat people camp in the Philippines at the age of seventeen in 1979. His first collection of six novelles was translated into French and published as Sous une pluie d'epines by Flammarion in 1998.
