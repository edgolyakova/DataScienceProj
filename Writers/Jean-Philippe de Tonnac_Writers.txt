Jean-Philippe de Tonnac is a French novelist, essayist, and journalist who directed the special editions of the Nouvel Observateur. He is the author of about twenty published books, among them "The Asexual Revolution" (2006). Publications Essays Qui vive ?, textes rassemblés en hommage à Julien Gracq, éd.
