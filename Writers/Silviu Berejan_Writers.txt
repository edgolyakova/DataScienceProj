Silviu Berejan (30 July 1927 – 10 November 2007) was a Romanian philologist, lexicologist and linguist from Bessarabia, one of the titular members of the Academy of Sciences of Moldova, the romance linguistic specialty (especially Romanian) and Slavic (especially Russian), principal scientist at the Institute of Linguistics of the Academy of Sciences of Moldova. Biography Silviu Berejan was born on 30 July 1927 in Bălăbăneşti, Criuleni. He graduated from the Moldova State University in 1952.
