Tata Esteban (born Steve Paolo Regala) (September 28, 1954 - September 10, 2003) was a Filipino producer-director. Educational life Esteban had a double major for his undergraduate degree in Business and Hotel and Restaurant Management obtained at the University of Baguio Filmography Director Actor Writer Production designer Producer Creative consultant Fight director Assistant director Composer Death Esteban died on September 10, 2003, from a heart attack.
