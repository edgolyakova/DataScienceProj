Lara Owen is an academic at the University of St Andrews. She researches and writes about menstruation and culture and menstruation.Owen has a PhD in Management & Organisation Studies from Monash University Business School in Melbourne. Her PhD investigated the uptake of menstrual workplace policies and menstrual cups.In 1993, Owen published the book Her Blood Is Gold: Awakening to the Wisdom of Menstruation. It has since been translated and re-published several times.
