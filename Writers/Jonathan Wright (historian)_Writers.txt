Jonathan Wright (born 1969) is a British historian and author. His books include The Jesuits: Missions, Myths and Histories (HarperCollins, 2004), published in the United States as God's Soldiers (Doubleday, 2004). He has recently published a book on heresy.Wright was educated at the Universities of St Andrews and Oxford where he was awarded a doctorate in 1999.
