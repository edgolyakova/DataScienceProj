Mimoza Ahmeti (born 12 June 1963) from Kruja is an Albanian woman poet. Works After two volumes of verse in the late eighties, it was the 53 poems in the collection Delirium, Tirana 1994 (Delirium), which caught the public's attention. Her most recent book is The Pollination of Flowers. Ahmeti has published widely and her books have been translated into Italian, French and English.
