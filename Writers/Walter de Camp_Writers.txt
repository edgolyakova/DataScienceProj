Walter de Camp (born Kari Lempinen; d. 5 April 2017) was a Finnish writer. He wrote about partying, restaurants and celebrities in the Finnish City magazine. There is also a drink named Walter de Camp after the writer. It was designed by Jasu Piasecki after the short description given by the writer: "It has to contain whiskey, be a short drink and taste really good."
