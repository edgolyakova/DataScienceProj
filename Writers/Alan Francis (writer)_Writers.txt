Alan Francis is a comedian and writer from Scotland. He is the co-author of play Jeffrey Dahmer is Unwell, which according to an otherwise unfavorable review in The Guardian was a "cult success" at the 1995 Edinburgh Festival premier, where Francis also co-starred. He also authored and starred in "Clobbered", which according to a positive 2004 review in The Independent is being adapted for both radio and television.In 1991, he won the Edinburgh Festival Fringe newcomer contest "So You Think You're Funny".
