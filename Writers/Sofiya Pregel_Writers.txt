Sofiya Yulievna Pregel (Russian: София Юльевна Прегель; 1894–1972) was a Russian poet. She emigrated to Berlin in 1922 and then to Paris in 1932, where she directed the Rifma publishing house.

Writings
1935. Razgovor s pamiat'iu. Paris: Chisla.
1937. Solnechnyi proizvol. Paris: Sovremennyie zapiski.
