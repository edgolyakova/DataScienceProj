Michaeley O'Brien is an Australian television writer, script editor and script producer. Born in Mudgee, she grew up in Gulgong and studied at Charles Sturt University in Bathurst as well as the Australian Film Television and Radio School. She worked as script producer on Water Rats and McLeod's Daughters and most recently wrote episodes of Underbelly: Razor.Mystery Road, Episode 1, Gone, was shortlisted for the NSW Premier's Literary Awards, Betty Rowland Prize for Scripwriting, 2019.
