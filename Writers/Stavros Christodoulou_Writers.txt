Stavros Christodoulou is a Cypriot writer. He was born in 1963 in Nicosia. He studied law in Athens, and worked as a journalist afterwards. He is a columnist for the leading Cypriot newspaper Phileleftheros. His first book Hotel National (2016) was shortlisted for the Cyprus State Literature Prize. His second book The Day the River Froze (2018) won the Cyprus State Literature Prize and the EU Prize for Literature. References
