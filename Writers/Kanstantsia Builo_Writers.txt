Kanstancyja Bujło (Belarusian: Канстанцыя Буйло) (1893–1986) was a Belarusian poet and playwright.Bujło's first publication in 1909 was Mound Flower, edited by Janka Kupała. The main themes of her poems, which often relied on folklore, were on peasant life in Belarus before 1917, and World War II heroism. One of her most notable poems was I Love Our Land.
