George Meniuc (20 May 1918, Chişinău – 8 February 1987, Chişinău) was a writer from Moldova. Biography George Meniuc was born on 20 May 1918, in Chişinău. He graduate from the University of Bucharest; his professors were Tudor Vianu, Petre P. Negulescu, Dimitrie Gusti, Mircea Florian. In 1940, he returned to Chişinău George Meniuc was editor in chief of Nistru, a newspaper of the Moldovan Writers' Union.George Meniuc died on 8 February 1987 and was buried in Chişinău. Awards Premiul de Stat al RSSM pentru literatură, 1972. "Scriitor al poporului din Moldova", 1982. Works Poems Cântecul zorilor (1948) Poezii (1954) Strofe lirice (1956) Poeme (1957) Versuri alese (1958) Vremea Lerului (1969), Florile dalbe (1979), Toamna lui Orfeu (1983).Essays: Imaginea în artă (1940), Iarba fiarelor (1959), Cadran solar (1966), Eseuri (1967).
