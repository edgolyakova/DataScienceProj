Konstantin "Kosta" Mandrović (Serbian Cyrillic: Коста Мандровић, fl. 1873–1903) was a Serbian illustrator, author and publisher in Vienna, Austria. He was an ethnic Serb who lived in Vienna. He was an experienced book trader before he started publishing the illustrated magazine of "Srpska zora" (1876-1881). Work Kosta Mandrović (1885).
