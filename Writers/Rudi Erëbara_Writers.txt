Rudi Erebara (born 1971) is an Albanian writer and translator. He studied at the Academy of Fine Arts in Tirana before becoming a journalist. He has published works of poetry as well as a couple of novels, namely Vezët e thëllëzave (Eggs of the Quails, 2010) and Epika e yjeve të mëngjesit (The Epic of the Morning Stars, 2016). He was awarded the EU Prize for Literature in 2017. References
