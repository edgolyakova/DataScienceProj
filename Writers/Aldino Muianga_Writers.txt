Aldino Muianga (born 1 May 1950) is a Mozambican physician and writer. Career Muianga was born in Lourenço Marques. He studied surgery and he is the national coordinator of Mozambican Community Work. He is presently a professor in the field of medicine in the University of Pretoria, South Africa. Personal life He is married to Carolina Teresa de Sousa. They have 3 children: Hugo Miguel Muianga, Aldino Frederico de Sousa Muianga, and Mick Ivan de Sousa Muianga. Awards Prémio Literário TDM, 2001 Prémio Literário Da Vinci, 2003 Prémio José Craveirinha de Literatura, 2009.
