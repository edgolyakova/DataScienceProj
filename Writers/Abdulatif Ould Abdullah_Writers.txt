Abdulatif Ould Abdullah is an Algerian writer. He was born in 1988 in the city of Mostaganem. He studied architecture at the University of Algiers. He has written three novels: Out of Control (2016) Flaunting Finery (2018) The Eye of Hammurabi (2020)He was shortlisted for the Arabic Booker Prize in 2021. References
