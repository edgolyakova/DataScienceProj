Abū al-Qāsim, Abd Allāh ibn Muḥammad ibn 'Abd Allāh al-'Azīz al-Baghawī (829CE - 929CE) (kunya: Ibn Bint Munī') was a jurist in Baghdad.  Al-Marzubānī was his pupil. Works Among his books were: Kitāb al-Mu’jam (كتاب المعجم) ‘Large Alphabetical Book’ ; Kitāb al-Mu’jam aṣ-Saghīīr (كتاب المعجم الصغير) ‘Small Alphabetical Book’;  Kitāb al-Musnad (كتاب المسند);  Kitāb as-Sann ‘alā madhahib al-fiqha (كتاب السنن على مذاهب الفقهآء) The Ordinances According to the Legal Systems of the Jurists.
