Marco Koskas is a French-Israeli writer.

Biography
His novel Balace Bounel won the Prix du Premier Roman award for young novelists in 1979.In 1980, he joins the Editions Grasset, thanks to Bernard-Henri Lévy, to publish Destino.In 2018, his novel Bande de Français, which was self-published on CreateSpace, was nominated for the Prix Renaudot.
