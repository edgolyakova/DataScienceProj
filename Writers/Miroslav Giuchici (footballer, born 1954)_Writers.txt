Miroslav Giuchici (born 18 April 1954) is a Romanian former footballer who played as a midfielder. His son who was also named Miroslav Giuchici was also a footballer. After he ended his playing career, Giuchici started his own football academy, which was called Srbianka Giuchici Timișoara. He wrote a book which was released in 2018 called Viață și fotbal (Life and football). Honours Politehnica Timișoara Divizia B: 1983–84, 1986–87 Cupa României runner-up: 1982–83 Notes References
