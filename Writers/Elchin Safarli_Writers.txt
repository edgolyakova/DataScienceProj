Elchin Safarli (Azerbaijani: Elçin Səfərli, Russian: Эльчин Сафарли; born 12 March 1984 in Baku, Azerbaijani SSR, USSR) is an Azerbaijani novelist and journalist. He has published ten novels, written and published in Russian. Composer Asya Sultanova set some of his work to music.
