François Le Peru (8 February 1940 – 13 January 2023), known by the pen name Fañch Peru, was a French teacher and writer of the Breton language. Biography Le Peru worked as a teacher in Tréguier for many years, where he was a member of the Cercle Culturel Ernest Renan. He wrote articles in numerous Breton-language newspapers and magazines, such as Brud, Ar Falz, and Le Peuple breton. As a member of the Breton Democratic Union, he served as mayor of Berhet from 1983 to 2001. Fañch Peru died on 13 January 2023, at the age of 82.
