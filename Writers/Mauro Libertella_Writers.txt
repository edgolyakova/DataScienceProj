Mauro Libertella (born 1983) is an Argentinian writer. He was born in Mexico City and grew up in Buenos Aires. He studied literature at university, before pursuing a career as a journalist and fiction writer. He has published five books to date. In 2017, he was named as one of the Bogota39, a list of the best young writers in Latin America.
