Yūichirō Oguro (小黒祐一郎, Oguro Yūichirō, born May 1, 1964) is a Japanese writer. He co-created the 1997 television anime series Revolutionary Girl Utena and its 1999 film sequel Adolescence of Utena as a member of the production group Be-Papas.
