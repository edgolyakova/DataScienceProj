Tensei Kono (河野 典生, Kōno Tensei, January 27, 1935 – January 29, 2012) was a Japanese science fiction writer. Among other works, he wrote Triceratops (トリケラトプス), which has been collected in The World Treasury of Science Fiction edited by David G. Hartwell. Works 1964 Black Sun.
