Klemens Ludwig (23 December 1955 – 12 December 2022) was a German astrologist, writer, publicist, and Tibetan scholar. He became first chairman of the Deutscher Astrologen-Verband in 2015.

Biography
After completing his secondary studies in Rüthen in 1975, Ludwig studied English and theology in Tübingen. In 1986, he visited Tibet for the first time and traveled there several times before his visa was refused by the Chinese government in 2004. However, he maintained his close ties with the Tibetan community in India, Nepal, and Switzerland and was president of the Tibet Initiative Deutschland from 1994 to 2000. He published numerous books and articles on Tibet.Suffering from amyotrophic lateral sclerosis, Ludwig died on 12 December 2022, at the age of 66.
