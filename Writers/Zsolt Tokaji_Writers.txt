Zsolt Tokaji (simplified Chinese: 陶凯; traditional Chinese: 陶凱; pinyin: Táo Kǎi: Wade–Giles: T'ao K'ai; born 25 March 1971 in Debrecen (Hungary) is a Hungarian writer, sinologist and translator chiefly known for translating many classic and early Chinese texts into Hungarian, most famously the Inner Canon of Huangdi. Biography Zsolt Tokaji received his MA degree from Eötvös Loránd University in 1999, (Chinese and Tibetan studies).
