Dubravko Horvatić (9 December 1939 – 20 May 2004) was a Croatian novelist, poet and essayist. Biography Dubravko Horvatić was born on 9 December 1939 in Zagreb. He finished Classical Gymnasium in Zagreb and enrolled Faculty of Humanities and Social Sciences in the same city. Horvatić wrote over fifty books. He was a member of Croatian Democratic Union. He died on 20 May 2004 in Zagreb at the age of 64. Works Horvatić's works have been translated into 25 languages.
