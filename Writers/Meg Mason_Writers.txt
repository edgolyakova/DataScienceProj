Meg Mason (born New Zealand) is an Australian author. Her novel Sorrow and Bliss, was short listed for the 2022 Women's Prize for Fiction.She was a journalist for Financial Times, Vogue and The New Yorker.She was influenced by Jane Austen, and George Saunders.
