Gray established a social policy research consultancy that contributed to public sector policy reports in New Zealand and other Pacific nations.In 1990, Gray received the New Zealand 1990 Commemoration Medal. In the 2003 Queen's Birthday Honours, she was awarded the Queen's Service Medal for public services.Gray died in Wellington from motor neuron disease on 1 September 2021.
