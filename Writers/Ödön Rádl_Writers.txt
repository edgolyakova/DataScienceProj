Ödön Rádl (March 30, 1856 - December 20, 1916) was a Hungarian writer and lawyer.He was born in Alsólugos (today Lugașu de Jos, Romania). He studied laws in Nagyvárad (today Oradea, Romania). He was a close friend of Kálmán and István Tisza. He wrote for several publications (Nagyváradi Lapok, Tiszavidék) and was a member of the Liberal Party and  Petőfi Társaság.
