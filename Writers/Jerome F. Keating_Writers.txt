Jerome F. Keating is an American writer and academic. Career He worked on the Taipei and Kaohsiung Mass Rapid Transit projects as the technology transfer manager. Keating first taught in Taiwan at Chinese Culture University before moving to National Taipei University. He is an established author in Taiwan known for his dislike of both the KMT and CCP.He regularly writes op-eds for the Taipei Times. He currently lives in Taipei, Taiwan.
