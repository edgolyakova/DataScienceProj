Mihail Dolgan (5 February 1939 – 2 May 2013) was a professor and researcher from Moldova, member of the Academy of Sciences of Moldova. Overview His speciality is the Romanian contemporary literature and he is a member of the "Department of Social Sciences and Humanities" of the Academy of Sciences of Moldova. Mihail Dolgan is the author of at least 50 books and more than 900 studies, articles, and literal chronics. His interests are focused on the fundamental themes and problems, as well as on integration, revaluation, of the postwar literature from the Republic of Moldova and Romania. Awards Medal „M. Eminescu” Medal Academy of Sciences of Moldova „D. Cantemir”.
