Domokos Bölöni (Daia, August 11, 1946) is a Romanian Magyar writer and journalist.After studying in Târnăveni, he studied the Romanian Hungarian language in Târgu Mureş. He has worked as a teacher in Corund and as a journalist for Népújság. He is married and has three children and three grandchildren.
