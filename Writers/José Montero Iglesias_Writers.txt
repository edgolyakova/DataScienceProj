José Montero Iglesias (born in 1878 – died on July 20, 1920) was a Spanish writer and journalist. Biography Jose Montero Iglesias was born in Ciudad Rodrigo, Salamanca, Spain and mostly lived in Santander and Madrid where he worked in newspapers and magazines. He was the director of the Cántabra Magazine and also collaborated in various newspapers and magazines such as La Atalaya , Letras Montañesas , El Diario Montañés , El Cantábrico , El Pueblo Cantabro. He was the father of José Montero Alonso , grandfather of José Montero Padilla. Death He died of tuberculosis at the young age of 42 in a sanatorium in Guadarrama. Works Notable Narrative works:Soledad (Loneliness), La sombra de Otelo (Othello's shadow), Carne y mármol (Meat and marble), Yelmo florido (Flowery Helm), El Solitario de Proaño (The Solitaire of Proano). Notable theater works:El patio de Monipodio (The courtyard of Monipodio) in collaboration with Francisco Moya Rico and with music by maestro Villa.
