Christian Marazzi (born 1951) is a Swiss economist and author. Biography He was born in Lugano, Switzerland in 1951. Education He graduated in political science from the University of Padua.He completed his master's degree in economics at the London School of Economics.He completed his Ph.D. in economics at the City University of London. Career He has held positions at the University of Lausanne, Padua, New York and Geneva, and is currently Professor and Research Director of Socio-Economics at the Scuola Universitaria della Svizzera Italiana.
