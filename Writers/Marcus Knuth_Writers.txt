Johan Henrik Marcus Knuth (born 2 April 1976 in Copenhagen) is a Danish politician, who is a member of the Folketing for the Conservative People's Party. He was elected into parliament in the 2015 Danish general election. Political career Knuth was elected into parliament for the Venstre party in the 2015 election, where he received 7,351 votes. He was reelected in 2019, where he received 9,523 votes.
