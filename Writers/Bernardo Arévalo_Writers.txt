César Bernardo Arévalo de León (born 7 October 1957) is a Guatemalan politician, diplomat, sociologist and writer. He has been a deputy in the Congress of Guatemala since 2020, previously he was Guatemalan Ambassador to Spain (1995–1996) and Deputy Minister of Foreign Affairs (1994–1995).Arévalo de León was born in 1957 in Guatemala City, the son of former Guatemalan President Juan José Arévalo Bermejo and his second wife, Margarita de León.
