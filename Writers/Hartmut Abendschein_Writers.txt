Hartmut Abendschein (Schwäbisch Hall, 7 October 1969) is a German – Swiss writer. Biography Hartmut Abendschein attended German studies and English studies in Stuttgart, Constance and Glasgow. He lives in Bern since 2003 where he has a blog called taberna kritika – kleine formen. Works Notula Nova – Theorie & Praxis (Auswahl). In: Idiome Nr. 4. Klever, Wien 2011.
