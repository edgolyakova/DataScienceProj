Khalid Hussain (Urdu: خالد حسین; born 1969 in Pakistan) is a Norwegian-Pakistani writer and film producer. Hussain moved to Norway, without his parents, in 1975. He came to attention after writing the book Pakkis in 1986. Hussain visits high schools to discuss his writing and life as an immigrant teenager.
