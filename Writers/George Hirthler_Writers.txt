George Hirthler is an American writer.  He is best known for writing the novel The Idealist, which earned him the Pierre de Coubertin medal from the International Olympic Committee.Hirthler began researching the Olympic Games in 1989. His novel The Idealist was published by the Ringworks Press in 2016. On June 23, 2022, he was awarded the Pierre de Coubertin medal along with the artist Rolf Lukaschewski. == References ==
