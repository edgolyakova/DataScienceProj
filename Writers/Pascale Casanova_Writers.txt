Pascale Casanova (February 14, 1959 – September 29, 2018) was a French literary critic. Life From 1997 to 2010, she was the author and editor of L'Atelier littéraire, a radio show on France Culture. She was a Visiting Professor in the Department of Romance Studies at Duke University. Works La republique mondiale des lettres, Paris : Editions du Seuil, 1999.
