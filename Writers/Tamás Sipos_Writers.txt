Tamás Sipos (15 December 1931 – 20 June 2002) was a Hungarian writer and sports commentator. His book of biographical fiction, Dohánybarna selyemmellény (1984), is based on his travails during the Nazi occupation of Hungary, during which he was imprisoned in a work camp and narrowly escaped death.
