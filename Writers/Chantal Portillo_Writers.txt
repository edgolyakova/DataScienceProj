Chantal Portillo (born 26 March 1957) is a French writer. She has published several books such as À mains nues (1995, winner of the prix Bleu-Citron), La Femmepluie (1999) and Journal d’une vieille sournoise et vilaine, en plus (2002) and Petite Punaise blanche (2005).She has also written a book on the disappeared Chadian politician Ibni Oumar Mahamat Saleh.
