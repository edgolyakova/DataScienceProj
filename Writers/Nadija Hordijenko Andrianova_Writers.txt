Nadia Hordijenko Andrianova (Надія Миколаївна Андріанова-Гордієнко in Ukrainian; 1921–1998) was a Ukrainian writer and translator of the language Esperanto. She studied literature and journalism in Kyiv and published articles and translations in Paco and Hungara Vivo. In 1987, the Hungarian Esperanto Association published her autobiography Vagante tra la mondo maltrankvila. She also wrote about Vasili Eroshenko.
