Riccardo Boglione (born 1970 in Genoa) is an Italian art curator, lecturer, writer, and art critic.He holds a PhD from the University of Pennsylvania. He specializes in avant-garde art and conceptual writing.Since 2006 he lives and works in Montevideo, Uruguay. Selected works Ritmo D.
