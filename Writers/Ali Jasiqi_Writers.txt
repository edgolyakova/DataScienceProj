Ali D. Jasiqi (born 1937 in Junik, Kingdom of Yugoslavia) is a Kosovan-Albanian writer. Education He attended schools in Junik and Deçan, Gjakovë, finishing in Pristina. He graduated from the Philological Faculty in Albanian Language and Literature at the University of Pristina. Current He continues to write for both printed and electronic media in Kosovo, Albania, Macedonia and elsewhere. He lives in Pristina with his wife Hasbije Jasiqi, son and daughter-in-law.
