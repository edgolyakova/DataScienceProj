Abu Bakr Hamid Khaal is an Eritrean writer. He is best known for his 2008 novel African Titanics which was translated into English by Charis Bredin. He has written a couple of other books, e.g. The Scent of Arms and Barkantiyya: Land of the Wise Woman. A member of the Eritrean Liberation Front who fought against the Ethiopian government, Khaal also lived in Libya for many years before moving to Denmark. == References ==
