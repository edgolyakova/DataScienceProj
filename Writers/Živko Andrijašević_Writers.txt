Živko M. Andrijašević  (born 24 February 1967, Bar, Montenegro) is a Montenegrin historian and writer. He is known for writing the standard historical dictionary of Montenegro. Works Pokrštavanje Muslimana 1913 godine (co-author with Zoran Stanojević), Published: Podgorica: Almanah, 2003. History of Montenegro: From Ancient Times to 2003; Part one Živko M. Andrijašević, part two Šerbo Rastoder. Published: Podgorica: Diaspora center, 2006.
