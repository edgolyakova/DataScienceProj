Karl Oskar Staffan Seeberg (Stockholm, August 4, 1938) is a Swedish writer and physician. He lives currently in Båstad.His first novel P:s lidanden was published in 1966, and his breakthrough came in 1970 with Vägen genom Vasaparken.He got his PhD in medicine in 1975. His works are very critical of the society.
