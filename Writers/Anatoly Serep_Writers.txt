Anatoly Trofimovich Serep (Chuvash & Russian: Сереп Анатолий Трофимович; 1920–2003), was a Chuvash writer and poet. Biography Serep was born 1920 in the village of Man Toktashin the Alikovsky Districtof the Chuvash Republic, Russia. He was educated in Kalinino (modern day Tashir), and studied pedagogy. He worked as a school teacher in the village Varmankassy of the Shumerlinsky District. In 1990, Serap became a member of the Union of the Writers of the Chuvash Republic.
