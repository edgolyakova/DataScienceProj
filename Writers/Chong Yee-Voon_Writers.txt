Chong Yee-Voon (Chinese: 鍾怡雯; pinyin: Zhōng Yíwén; Jyutping: Zung1 Ji4 Man4; Pe̍h-ōe-jī: Cheng Î-bûn; Pha̍k-fa-sṳ: Chûng Yì-vùn, Kampar, Perak, February 13, 1969) is a Malaysian writer in the Chinese language. She is professor of the Department of Chinese Linguistics and Literature in Yuan Ze University, Taiwan.She studied at the National Taiwan Normal University.
