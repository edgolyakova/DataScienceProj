Makwaia wa Kuhenga is  a Tanzanian columnist, author and a television presenter.

Early life and career
In 1991, he graduated from the University of Wales in Cardiff with an MA in journalism.

Publications
Kumbe mke huyu mchawi: matukio ya kweli [Alas The Wife is A Witch: true story] (in Swahili). Je, Tutafika? Publications. 2005. ISBN 9789987905270.
CCM na mustakabali wa nchi yetu [CCM and the future of our country] (in Swahili). Je, Tutafika? Publications. 2007. ISBN 9789987905263.
