Borislav "Mihiz" Mihajlović (Serbian Cyrillic: Борислав Михајловић Михиз; 17 October 1922 – 15 December 1997) was a Serbian writer and literary critic.He was one of the leaders of the Committee for the Protection of Artistic Freedom.A street in Dorćol is named after him.
