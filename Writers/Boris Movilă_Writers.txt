Boris Movilă (born October 6, 1928 in Hîjdieni) is a writer from the Republic of Moldova. He was born in Hîjdieni (Berezlogi, Orhei District). Movilă was the deputy president of the Comitetul pentru Cinematografie and redactor in chief of Moldova-Film. He is a leader of the Democratic Forum of Romanians in Moldova.
