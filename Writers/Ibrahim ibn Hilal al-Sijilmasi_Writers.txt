Abu Ishak Ibrahim ibn Hilal al-Sijilmasi (Arabic: إبراهيم بن هلال السجلماسيdied circa 1497-1498) was a Moroccan legal scholar well known for his verdicts (fatwas). He is the author of al-Durr al-nathīr alá Ajwibat Abī al-Hasan al-Saghīr, Hādhihi ajwibat al-Imām ibn Hilāl  and a commentary on Misbah al-arwah fi usul al falah.
