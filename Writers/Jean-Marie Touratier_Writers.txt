Jean-Marie Touratier (1943 – 1 February 2021) was a French writer, author, and artistic director. Biography Touratier earned an agrégation in modern literature and subsequently became the director of the Fonds régional d'art contemporain in Toulouse and arranged multiple art exhibitions. He then worked as a cultural advisor to the Rector of Paris, serving as an academic delegate for arts and culture. In addition to his career as an artistic director, he wrote a number of books about different works of art. Jean-Marie Touratier died in Paris on 1 February 2021 at the age of 77 from COVID-19.
