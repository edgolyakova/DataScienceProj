Abdukadir Osman (Somali: Cabdulkaadir Cismaan, Arabic: عبد القادر عثمان), also known as Abdukadir Oromo, was a Somali writer. He was born and spent most of his life in Somalia, especially the capital Mogadishu. Osman wrote numerous books, most of which are centered on local politics.
