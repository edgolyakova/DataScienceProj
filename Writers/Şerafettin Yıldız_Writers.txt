Şerafettin Yıldız (born 1953) is a Turkish-Austrian writer and translator. He was born in Sürmene, Turkey, but has lived and worked in Austria since 1978. He writes mainly books for children and teenagers. He has also published poetry in both Turkish language and German language. Works Herzfinsternis. Gedichte. Baden: Grasl 1998 Der himmelblaue Gruß.
