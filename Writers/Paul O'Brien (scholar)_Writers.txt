He was the first Professor of Irish at St. Patrick's College, Maynooth, serving from 1802 until his death.Born in 1763, from Cormeen, Co. Meath, O'Brien was a great grand nephew of Turlough O'Carolan.In 1807 O'Brien was instrumental in establishing The Gaelic Society of Dublin along with Dr. John Lanigan, Edward O'Reilly, William Halliday, and Theophilus O'Flanagan. Professor O'Brien was also involved in Iberno-Celtic Society another initiative to promote the Irish Language.
