Marco Schank (born 10 October 1954 in Ettelbruck) is a Luxembourgian politician for the Christian Social People's Party (CSV) and author.  He is a member of the national legislature, the Chamber of Deputies, representing the Nord constituency since 2 February 1999.He has been the mayor of Heiderscheid since 1 January 1994.  Prior to that, he was a councillor in Heinerscheid (1982–1993).Schank is the President of the Upper Sûre Natural Park, and has been President of the National Tourism Office since 13 March 2000 to 2009.
