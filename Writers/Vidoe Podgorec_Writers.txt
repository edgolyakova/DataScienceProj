Vidoe Podgorec (Macedonian: Видое Подгорец; 8 June 1934 – 14 April 1997) was a Macedonian writer and poet. He was born in Kolešino near Strumica and lived in Skopje. He wrote for children and adults and published a great number of poems, stories and novels.
One of his most famous novels is The White Gypsy (Белото циганче).
