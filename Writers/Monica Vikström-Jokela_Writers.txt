Monica Vikström-Jokela (born 19 June 1960) is a Finnish-Swedish television script writer and author from Finland.She lives in Nuuksio, Espoo with her five children. She has written four books about "Ellen Annorlunda" in Swedish, and one fact book "Kyrkoåret runt i skola och hem". The Ellen Annorlunda (translated: Ellen the different one) books have been translated into Finnish and Norwegian.
