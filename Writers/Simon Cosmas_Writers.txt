Simon Cosmas Michael also recognized by his pen name Simon Rieber, is a Tanzanian visual artist and author. He has exhibited widely in Tanzania and East Africa, building a niche as an emerging contemporary Tanzanian artist on the East Africa art scene. In 2020 he won Umoja Prize for Contemporary Tanzanian Artist. He is the author of Beautiful Monster his first novel for adults, and I will never forget you a two-volume an ongoing crime fiction series. Bibliography Books Exhibitions 2021: Look at me, Museum of contemporary art, Moscow, Russia. 2019: Pinchuk Art Center, Kiev, Ukraine. 2018: UFO Gallery, San Francisco.
