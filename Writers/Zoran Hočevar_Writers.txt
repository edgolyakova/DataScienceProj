Zoran Hočevar (born 21 December 1944) is a Slovene writer, playwright and painter.
He won the Kresnik Award for his novel Šolen z Brega in 1998.

Novels
Porkasvet (1995)
Šolen z Brega (1997)
Za znoret (1999)
Rožen cvet (2004)
Ernijeva kuhna (2010)

Plays
Smeči (1995)
Mož za Zofijo (1998)
M' te ubu (2001)
