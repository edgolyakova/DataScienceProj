Jakob Margido Esp (born 7 June 1943) is a Norwegian actor and writer. He is particularly known for his drag character "Flettfrid Andresen".He was awarded the Leonard Statuette in 2009. Personal life Esp was born in Byneset (later merged with Trondheim) in 1943, a son of farmer Aksel Esp and Agnes Ervik. Selected works Flettfrid (LP, 1984) Opp alle jordens velsoignerte.
